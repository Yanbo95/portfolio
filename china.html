Shanghai Wild Animal Park is one of the first AAAA national visiting spots. It provides great opportunities for tourists to get close to more than 200 kinds representative and rare animals form the world, such as foreign giraffe, zebra, white rhinoceros and so on, at the same time such as national prior protected giant panda, snub-nosed monkey, Chinese tiger. I was feeding on the giraffe with branches. They were very friendly and mild.

Yellow Crane Tower is located at the top of Snake Hill, Wuhan. It is one of Four Major Towers in China, which is 51.4 meters high with five storeys. Poets in the past dynasties have written many immortal poems here. Standing at the top of the tower, people were easily to expresses homesick and ambitions. Yellow Crane Tower consists of the main tower, Pei Pavilion, veranda with windows, memorial archway, Nanou Tower, and veranda with poem styles. Although it has been repaired and rebuilt for 10 times, I still felt the culture and history behind three Kingdoms Period.

Waitan is a famous waterfront on the west bank of Huangpu River. It regarded as the symbol of Shanghai. As we can see the icon represents Shanghai in many places. The charm of Shanghai as a bustling metropolis combining the century-old history and flourishing future is fully presented. On the other side of the river, the Oriental Pearl Tower is lit among hundreds of international financial institutions.

Wuhan Haichang Polar Ocean World is a large indoor polar ocean theme park in China. It was great experience to watch the shows of south and north polar marine animals, such as emperor penguins, polar bears, white whales, walrus and dolphins. Wuhan Haichang Polar Ocean World provides a window of looking into the ocean and experiencing style.
